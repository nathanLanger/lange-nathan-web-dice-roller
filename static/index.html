Welcome to Dice Roller!

Instructions:

Roll the dice to defeat the dark army. To fight roll the dice.
Players are then allowed 2 rerolls on their turn. First, they 
select the dice they wish to save and not reroll by clicking
the save button, which will switch to unsave so the player
may unsave that die's roll if they so choose to. Second, they 
select roll to reroll all dice that were not saved. To win, players 
must reduce the dragon's health to 0. When it is the start of a 
player's turn they are to toggle all save buttons to off and roll.

Dice Roll Meanings:

Pair (two dice of the same number) deals 2 stab damage.
Full House (two dice of the same number and three of another)
deals 4 push damage and prevents the player from receiving 
stab damage until their next turn. 
Straight (five dice that range from 1 to 5 or 2 to 6) deals 6
magic damage.
Five-of-a-Kind (five dice of the same number) deals 10 stab
damage.
